About Me

Hello! My name is Michael Gervasoni and I'm a full stack software developer. I was born in NJ and was a curious and creative kid. As a teenager I taught myself some html so I could make my own websites and got into making flash websites.

After highschool, I went to college as a music major, dropped out, worked a variety of jobs, started my own businesses and then went back to school for engineering. I'm proud to say I graduated Summa Cum Laude from UCLA with a B.S. in Materials Engineering.

After working as an engineer in the LA area for over a year, I moved to San Diego to be closer to family and to find a career with more growth potential. I started looking into software development, fell in love with coding, and the rest is history.
